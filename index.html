About Me

Hi! I'm Amèlia, a graphic designer with a deep love for travel, discovery, and storytelling. From colorful city streets to quiet mountain trails, I believe every destination has a story worth telling; and I’m here to capture and share those stories with you.

Top Destinations

  1. Amsterdam, Netherlands

    A city of canals, bicycles, and creativity. Whether you're exploring the Van Gogh Museum or sipping coffee along the Jordaan, Amsterdam always feels alive.

  2. Victoria, Canada

    Coastal charm meets wild Pacific beauty. This island city is full of vibrant gardens, historic buildings, and calming sea breezes.

  3. Iparraldea, France

    The northern Basque Country is a treasure of tradition, coastal towns, and culture. From the markets of Bayonne to the beaches of Biarritz, it’s an unforgettable mix of French and Basque life.

  4. Idyllwild, USA

    A pine-scented escape in Southern California’s mountains. Artists, hikers, and those in need of quiet retreat find peace in this charming forest town.

  5. Andorra la Vella, Andorra

    Nestled in the Pyrenees, this tiny capital offers a blend of modern boutiques, medieval streets, and snow-covered slopes. A true hidden gem between France and Spain.
