Cat Plaza: My Feline-Filled Stay in San Juan

When I booked my Airbnb in San Juan, Puerto Rico, I anticipated vibrant culture, historic charm, and ocean breezes. What I didn't expect was to find myself living next to what I affectionately dubbed "Cat Plaza."

From the moment I arrived, I noticed cats lounging on sunlit steps, weaving through the cobblestone streets, and peering from balconies. These weren't just a few strays; they were a community of felines that seemed to have claimed this part of the city as their own.

Each morning, I'd sip my coffee while watching the cats stretch and greet the day. In the evenings, they'd gather in the plaza, creating a serene, almost magical atmosphere. Their presence added a unique rhythm to the neighborhood, blending seamlessly with the historic ambiance of Old San Juan.

Staying next to "Cat Plaza" became one of the highlights of my trip. It offered a glimpse into the harmonious coexistence between the residents of San Juan and their feline neighbors, adding depth and warmth to my travel experience.
